Our Process

How We Work
From Concept to Completion

Our systematic approach ensures smooth project execution and exceptional results. Every step is designed to keep you informed and involved.

01

Initial Consultation

We start with a detailed discussion about your requirements, style preferences, budget, and timeline. Our designer visits your space for measurements and assessment.

  • Free site visit and consultation
  • Requirement analysis
  • Budget discussion
  • Style preference assessment
Duration: 1-2 Days
02

Design Proposal

Based on your inputs, we create initial design concepts with floor plans, mood boards, and material suggestions. We present multiple options for your review.

  • Multiple design concepts
  • Floor plan layouts
  • Color schemes and materials
  • Detailed cost estimation
Duration: 3-5 Days
03

3D Visualization

Once you approve the design concept, we create detailed 3D renders and walkthroughs so you can visualize the final result before execution begins.

  • Photorealistic 3D renders
  • Virtual walkthrough
  • Material and lighting preview
  • Design refinements
Duration: 5-7 Days
04

Project Execution

With approved designs and materials, our skilled team begins the execution. We coordinate all trades and provide regular updates on progress.

  • Skilled craftsmen and contractors
  • Quality material procurement
  • Daily progress updates
  • Regular quality checks
Duration: As per project scope
05

Handover & Support

Final inspection, cleanup, and handover of your beautifully designed space. We provide maintenance guidelines and ongoing support for any issues.

  • Final quality inspection
  • Professional cleaning
  • Maintenance guidelines
  • 2-year warranty coverage
Duration: 1-2 Days
